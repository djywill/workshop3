Frequently Asked Questions

Are you available for research collaboration?

Yes, I'm always interested in collaborating on research projects related to educational technology, AI in education, immersive learning environments, and related fields. Please reach out via email with your collaboration proposal.

Do you accept PhD students?

I occasionally co-supervise PhD students in areas related to my research expertise. If you're interested in pursuing doctoral research in EdTech, please send your CV and research proposal to discuss potential opportunities.

Can I invite you to speak at an event?

I'm happy to consider speaking invitations for conferences, workshops, and seminars related to educational technology and AI in education. Please provide details about the event, audience, and topic in your inquiry.

How long does it take to respond to inquiries?

I typically respond to emails within 2-3 business days during the academic term. During busy periods or holidays, responses may take slightly longer. Thank you for your patience.

Ready to Start a Conversation?

I look forward to connecting with fellow researchers, educators, and anyone passionate about educational technology.
