About Me

Passionate about transforming education through technology.

Biography

I am currently an Educational Development Officer at the Centre for Learning, Teaching and Technology, the Education University of Hong Kong. With over a decade of experience in educational technology, I have dedicated my career to exploring how emerging technologies can transform learning experiences.

My journey in EdTech began with a deep curiosity about how technology could bridge gaps in traditional education. This curiosity led me to pursue advanced studies in AI and Educational Technology, where I developed a strong foundation in both theoretical frameworks and practical applications.

Today, I focus on leveraging generative AI, VR/AR, and robotics as scaffolds to enhance students' performance and engagement. I believe that technology should not replace traditional teaching but augment it, creating hybrid learning environments that prepare students for the future.
