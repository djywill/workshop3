Research Areas

Exploring the frontiers of educational technology.

My research focuses on leveraging emerging technologies—generative AI, VR/AR, and robotics—as cognitive scaffolds to enhance learning experiences and outcomes.

Generative AI & LLMs

Investigating how large language models and generative AI can serve as intelligent scaffolds in educational contexts. My work explores prompt engineering, AI-assisted content creation, and the development of AI-powered tutoring systems.

ChatGPT LLM Fine-tuning Prompt Engineering

Immersive Learning Environments

Creating virtual and augmented reality experiences that transport learners to otherwise inaccessible environments. From historical recreations to scientific simulations, immersive technology offers unprecedented learning opportunities.

Unity 3D Metaverse CAVE Systems

Educational Robotics

Exploring how robotics can enhance STEM education and develop computational thinking skills. My research investigates human-robot interaction in learning contexts and the design of robotics curricula for diverse learner populations.

Human-Robot Interaction STEM Education Physical Computing

Game-Based Learning

Designing educational games that balance engagement with pedagogical effectiveness. My work examines gamification strategies, serious games development, and the cognitive impacts of game-based learning environments.

Gamification Serious Games Academic Integrity
